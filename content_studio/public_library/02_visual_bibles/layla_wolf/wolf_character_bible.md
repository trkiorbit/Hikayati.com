# Character Bible — الذئب الحكيم (the_wise_wolf)
**Story:** ليلى والذئب الذكي
**Role:** supporting character — teaches Layla not to judge by appearance

---

## character_name
- **AR:** الذئب الحكيم
- **EN:** The Wise Wolf
- **note_for_image_generator:** use "a gentle gray wolf" — never "scary wolf", "big bad wolf", "hunting wolf"

---

## species & age
- **species:** gray wolf (forest dweller, not arctic)
- **apparent_age:** middle-aged adult wolf (calm, experienced)
- **size:** medium — slightly larger than a large dog, never monstrous

---

## appearance

### fur
- **primary_color:** warm gray (not pure gray, not silver, not black)
- **chest & belly:** lighter cream gray
- **texture:** soft, neat, well-groomed (not matted, not wild)

### eyes
- **color:** soft amber / warm honey brown
- **shape:** gentle, almond
- **expression:** calm, wise, kind (NEVER predatory, NEVER glowing, NEVER red)

### face
- **muzzle:** normal wolf proportions, not elongated menacingly
- **ears:** alert but relaxed, pointed naturally
- **mouth:** usually closed in a calm expression
- **when_open:** slight friendly panting, tongue visible briefly, NOT a snarl

### body
- **posture:** usually sitting or standing naturally, calm
- **tail:** held in a relaxed curve, NEVER tucked (fearful) or raised high (aggressive)
- **paws:** 4 paws, normal wolf anatomy

---

## personality (shows through expression)
- **wise** — serene, knowing expression
- **gentle** — soft eyes, non-threatening posture
- **patient** — still body language, not pacing
- **helpful** — slight head tilt when listening to Layla
- **misunderstood** — dignified, not showing hurt openly

## forbidden_visuals

❌ **NEVER depict the wolf as:**
- ❌ baring teeth (no snarling, no fangs exposed)
- ❌ snarling or growling (no scary mouth shapes)
- ❌ glowing or red eyes
- ❌ crouching in predator stance
- ❌ pouncing or running at Layla aggressively
- ❌ with blood on fur or mouth
- ❌ with prey in mouth
- ❌ shadowed eyes (gives villain look)
- ❌ oversized, monstrous scale
- ❌ surrounded by dark/red fog (horror tropes)
- ❌ standing on hind legs unnaturally
- ❌ wearing clothes (not anthropomorphic)
- ❌ with cartoon "evil" smirk

### Always depict as:
- ✅ calm, sitting or standing naturally
- ✅ soft, kind expression
- ✅ normal forest setting with warm light
- ✅ natural fur tones
- ✅ friendly body language (relaxed tail, alert ears)
- ✅ near Layla in a non-threatening way — at her level or slightly lower

## Scene Appearance Guide

### Scene 1: NOT present (Layla at home)
### Scene 2: NOT present (only wolf tracks visible on forest ground)
### Scene 3: First appearance — sitting calmly at a distance, kind eyes, head slightly tilted
### Scene 4: Closer, showing wolf pointing (with nose or paw) toward a broken bridge or danger sign
### Scene 5: Cooperating with Layla — both working together, wolf helping in a gentle way
### Scene 6: NOT present (Layla with Grandmother at her cottage)

---

## Final Note for Prompt Engineer

The wolf is the **emotional center** of the story — he must feel like a trusted friend, not a threat.
Every image must communicate: "This wolf is safe. He is a friend. You can trust him."

If the generated image has ANY menacing quality, reject and regenerate with stronger "gentle / friendly" keywords.
